About Us
YESE saves small business managers up to 30% in electrical costs through our low cost energy-efficiency services in commercial buildings.

We use best in class energy assessment techniques IoT software and web-based energy management dashboards to accurately (99%) monitor and optimize electrical power systems. We provide financial advisory services to enable our clients leverage identified opportunities. We also provide ongoing project management and real-time electrical energy monitoring and visualization as well as engagement in long-term power cost reduction through behavior change.

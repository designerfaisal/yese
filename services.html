Our Services
AUDITS & BENCHMARKING
We undertake electrical energy audits and benchmarking (EPA Energy Star) to identify specific opportunities for energy efficiency projects in commercial buildings. Our audits are transparent and reliable because we ensure our clients have a clear picture of their electrical energy system and its associated energy-efficiency opportunities.
FINANCING ADVISORY
We undertake financial modelling to identify return on investment scenarios for energy efficiency projects. We also support our clients to secure low-cost financial closure of identified energy-efficiency project opportunities exploring all available opportunities with federal and state energy-efficiency rebate, incentive programs as well as commercial banks.
PROJECT MANAGEMENT
We provide energy efficiency project management to ensure proper installation, ongoing maintenance and maintenance of energy efficiency solutions.
ENERGY MONITORING & DATA VISUALIZATION
We provide timely access to real-time and accurate (99%) energy use data using internet of things (IoT) dashboards across the entire commercial building by equipment category. This data is key for the proactive management of energy use in buildings as well as identifying and trouble-shooting electrical equipment issues before they become major problems.
GHG MANAGEMENT
YESE Energy services also provides cradle to grave Green House Gas (GHG) gas management using the latest climate science. We undertake measurement, verification and reporting of GHG and manage carbon offsets using the best tools and mechanisms including; Green House Gas protocol, Renewable Energy Credits (RECs); Science Base Targets and other similar tools and initiatives.

All our services are complimented with a dedicated team of certified YESE Energy Advisor who ensure that technological improvements are sustained through ongoing commercial building user engagement, education and support.

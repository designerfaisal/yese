Our Team
ROBERT DDAMULIRA
Founder & CEO
After 15 years working in the energy and environmental sustainability space including 9 years with the World-Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) Robert realized the important opportunity energy efficiency provides to meeting the dual challenge of sustaining economic development and addressing climate change, the generational challenge of our time. During 2018, U.S, the electric power sector accounted for about 38% of U.S. primary energy consumption, and produced 33% of total U.S. energy-related CO2 emissions, yet more than 65% of the electrical power generated in 2018 was never used; but wasted due to energy inefficiency (EIA, 2019). Commercial buildings sector account for 40% of total energy consumption, higher than transportation and industrial sectors (EIA, 2016) and present an important opportunity for realizing energy efficiency. It is based on this knowledge and practical experience that Robert founded YESE Energy to provide practical solutions needed by business, people and mother Earth. Robert is currently completing his PhD in Energy and Environmental Policy (ABD) at the University of Delaware and provides day today leadership as the founder and CEO of YESE Energy.

TOM ROBERTS
Advisor
Tom Roberts works in the Innovation department of IGS Energy, developing next generation energy management and information solutions for IGS Energy. Prior to joining IGS, Tom started his own business, PowerSage, which was acquired by IGS in 2018. Tom started PowerSage to help people answer the question “Why is my utility bill so high?” As Senior Director of Operations at Nationwide Energy Partners - a utility billing company serving more than 25,000 apartments. Tom is a naturally curious individual that focuses on the possibilities of situations and thrives on designing solutions to complex problems. His engineering background instills a methodical and systematic approach to problem solving activities, while passions for customer service and branding guide his interactions. Tom credits his military experience for his foundation in electronics, and more importantly a disciplined, systematic approach to procedures. Tom received a Bachelor of Science in Metallurgical Engineering from The Ohio State University.
Prof. KALIM SHAH
Advisor
Prof. Shah is a professor at the University of Delaware, Energy and Environmental Policy Department. He has eighteen years of policy analysis and development experience working as a consultant and researcher in the fields of climate change, energy and corporate sustainability. His experiences include implementing national and regional projects on climate change mainstreaming, industrial policy evaluation and sustainability investments. He has held senior policy positions with the Governments of Trinidad & Tobago and Canada, Caribbean Central American Action and faculty appointments at top tier University of Delaware (present), Indiana University, York University and the University of the West Indies and has completed over twenty projects and studies in the Caribbean, Central America and South East Asia. He has conducted regulatory impact assessments, cost effectiveness assessments and multi-criteria assessments. He has also designed and implemented industry, organizational and community surveys and various climate vulnerability, environmental performance and social protection indices. His research work has been published in over twenty five peer reviewed journal articles on climate change and energy policy, environmental management and corporate social responsibility. A Fulbright Scholar, he holds a Doctorate in Public Policy focused on environmental policy and Masters in Natural Resource Management focused on resource economics.
